Beat to Burn Fat and Lose Weight

Ingredients:

  • Grapefruit
  • Nopal

Recipe:

First you must wash the fruits properly, you must squeeze the grapefruit juice in two glasses, add it to the blender, the cactus should be peeled carefully, remove the thorns without removing its bark, then add in the blender and mix with the juice Of grapefruit until obtaining a uniform mixture, to serve and to enjoy.
