Apple Shake

The apple is excellent for cleaning the body and helping to burn fat. With this shake you will help by strengthening your muscles and burning fat.

How to prepare:

Add to blender:

  • 1 cup light natural yogurt.
  • 1 apple cut into pieces.
  • 1 teaspoonful of pure honey.
  • 1/2 cup almonds.
  • Cinnamon
  • 1/2 liter of skim milk.
Blend everything and the healthy smoothie is ready.
