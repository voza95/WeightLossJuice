Spice Infusion

With this green tea infusion plus the benefits of tea, you will have the benefits of pepper and cinnamon to further accelerate your metabolism and honey by cleansing your body.

How to prepare:

  • Serve a cup of green tea.
  • Add a bit of honey.
  • Add a pinch of cayenne pepper, less amount than honey.
  • Add one teaspoon ground cinnamon.
